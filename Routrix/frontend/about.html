The Origin of Routrix

Suraj Jha is the Founder and Owner of Routrix, established on 12/05/2025. Headquartered in Rohini, New Delhi, Routrix reflects his ambition to create a scalable, technology-driven ecosystem.

Pursuing a Bachelor in Computer Applications (BCA), he integrates Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to solve modern logistics challenges. His vision extends across India and into global markets including France, Dubai, and Russia, Routrix provides end-to-end logistics solutions, including packing, transportation, dispatch handling, and labour support services. With a strong emphasis on precision and execution, every shipment is managed through a structured workflow—ensuring safety, timely delivery, and complete transparency.

“At Routrix, we don’t just move goods—we take responsibility for every delivery with trust, precision, and commitment.”
