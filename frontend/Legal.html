Legal Segment 01

Terms & Conditions

01 Nature of Services

Routrix operates exclusively as a high-performance logistics coordination platform that bridges the gap between commercial clients and transport service providers for the movement of goods across India. The company currently focuses on business-to-business (B2B) logistics services and does not directly provide packers and movers or residential shifting services. Our role is primarily that of a facilitator, utilizing advanced coordination protocols to ensure that shipments are managed with maximum efficiency between pickup and delivery points.

02 User Eligibility

By utilizing the Routrix platform, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age and possess the legal capacity to enter into a binding agreement. Users are strictly required to provide accurate, complete, and verifiable information during the booking process and all subsequent communications. Any misuse of the platform—including but not limited to providing fraudulent details or engaging in illegal activities—will result in immediate termination of service.

03 Booking & Dispatch Policy

All booking requests placed through the Routrix ecosystem are subject to vehicle availability and internal administrative confirmation. To ensure security and prevent ghost bookings, Routrix conducts a mandatory reconfirmation protocol with the client via registered contact channels before any vehicle is dispatched to the pickup site. A booking is only deemed "Final" once this reconfirmation is successful.

04 Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation policy is designed to address the logistical overhead of mobilizing transport assets. Once goods are dispatched, they cannot be recalled unless under extraordinary circumstances explicitly agreed upon in writing with Routrix management. Furthermore, if the vehicle is already en route or has arrived at the designated pickup location, additional charges covering the distance traveled, mobilization time, and fuel consumption will be billed to the client at current operational rates.

05 Payment Security

All payments must be processed exclusively through the official Routrix bank accounts. Clients are strictly prohibited from making any direct payments to drivers or agents. Every Routrix field employee carries an official ID card with a unique, verified QR code—clients must verify this ID before completing any transaction. All payments must be strictly in the name of "ROUTRIX".

06 Verification & Documents

Routrix does not publicly display full GST or MSME registration details for security reasons. Clients requiring comprehensive verification for internal compliance are invited to visit the Routrix regional offices in person to inspect all legitimate documentation and regulatory certificates.

07 Billing & GST

Invoices detail GST rates and service fees. Penal interest of 24% per annum is applied to payments delayed beyond 15 days. Detailed billing summaries are provided upon successful delivery and verification of the consignment.

Legal Segment 02

Privacy Policy

Privacy, Data & Platform Usage

At Routrix, the privacy and security of our users is a top priority. By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information strictly for operational, communication, and service improvement purposes.

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, business information, pickup and delivery addresses, and transaction data. Routrix does not sell, rent, or misuse user data. However, certain information may be shared with transport partners or third-party service providers strictly for the purpose of fulfilling logistics operations.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Routrix may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, store preferences, and analyze platform usage. These technologies help us understand how you interact with our portal, allowing us to optimize performance. Users can choose to disable cookies through their browser settings; however, some features of the platform may not function properly as a result.

Fraud Prevention & Security

Routrix actively monitors activities to prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and misuse of the platform. We employ robust digital monitoring to detect suspicious behavior, including fake bookings, payment manipulation, or misuse of company identity. Any such activity will lead to immediate account suspension and the initiation of formal legal action.

User Responsibility & Safe Usage

Users are expected to use Routrix responsibly and ensure that all provided information is accurate. Routrix does not knowingly collect sensitive personal data beyond what is required for logistics operations. It is the user's duty to maintain the confidentiality of their booking details and interaction logs.

Global Compliance Mindset

While Routrix primarily operates in India, we follow standard digital practices aligned with global data protection principles to ensure transparency, accountability, and trust. Our internal protocols are designed to treat user data with the highest level of professional care, mirroring international standards for B2B logistics data handling.

Legal Segment 03

Employee Code of Conduct

📝 Official Statement

Effective Date: 12-05-2025

This document outlines the professional standards, responsibilities, operational guidelines, and data security practices for all individuals associated with Routrix. The objective of this policy is to create a safe, transparent, and efficient working environment while maintaining the highest level of trust and satisfaction for our clients.

By joining Routrix, every employee agrees to uphold the company’s core values of honesty, responsibility, discipline, and respect. This policy is not only a set of rules but a mutual commitment—Routrix ensures support, safety, and growth opportunities, while employees ensure professional conduct and service excellence.

👥 Scope of Employment

This policy applies to all individuals working with Routrix, including drivers, labour staff, supervisors, operation managers, support teams, and any future roles introduced as the company expands. It is applicable to all employment types, including permanent, temporary, contract-based, freelance, and partner roles. Every individual associated with Routrix represents the company and is expected to maintain professionalism at all times.

🪪 Verification & Onboarding

To ensure safety, authenticity, and accountability, all employees must complete a mandatory verification process. Each employee is required to submit valid government-issued identification and current address proof. Drivers must additionally provide a valid driving license and relevant vehicle documents. Routrix reserves the right to verify all submitted documents and may reject or suspend onboarding in case of incomplete, invalid, or suspicious information.

🔐 Data Security & Client Privacy

Routrix handles sensitive information such as client details, addresses, and shipment data. All employees are strictly required to maintain confidentiality and use this data only for official work purposes. Employees must not share, misuse, or access data beyond their assigned role. Unauthorized use of the driver portal or internal data is strictly prohibited. Any breach will result in immediate termination and potential legal action.

💰 Payment Rules & Financial Discipline

All client payments must be made only through official Routrix payment channels. Employees must not accept direct payments, request extra charges, or negotiate pricing outside official orders. If a client attempts to make a direct payment, the employee must guide them to official methods. Salaries will be transferred directly to registered bank accounts.

📲 Trip Management & Tracking

Accurate tracking and timely updates are essential. Whenever a Trip ID or Tracking ID is assigned, employees must ensure that all updates are correct, real-time, and honest. False or misleading updates can damage client trust and negatively impact company reputation.

Verification Protocol

Employees must confirm the identity of the receiver through OTP or other approved verification methods before handing over goods.

🚑 Emergency Support & Behavior

Employees are expected to maintain a polite, respectful, and professional attitude. In case of issues, avoid arguments and immediately inform the support team. Routrix prioritizes safety; in case of accidents or breakdowns, inform the support team immediately for assistance.

⭐ Performance & Discipline

Employees are assessed on punctuality, behavior, work accuracy, and tracking updates. High-performing employees may receive incentives and growth opportunities. Conversely, Routrix follows a zero-tolerance policy for fraud, theft, or misuse of company systems, which will result in removal and legal consequences.

🚪 Exit Policy

Employees who wish to leave Routrix must provide at least one week’s prior notice along with a valid reason to ensure a smooth transition. Leaving without notice is considered a violation of company policy.

Your continued use of the Routrix platform, including booking services, interacting with our system, or communicating with our team, shall be considered as your full and unconditional acceptance of these terms. If you do not agree with any part of these Terms, you are advised not to use the Routrix platform or its services.
